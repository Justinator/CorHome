Complete Home Assessments

All assessments are completed on site by an occupational therapy professional who collaborates with both medical staff and licensed contractors.

  • Sensitive to timelines and discharge dates
  • Measurements, drawings and photos of current home
  • Professional floor plan designs for various options
  • Medical and functional necessity is top consideration
  • Understanding of durable medical equipment in the home
  • Alliance with carrier to determine desired outcomes
  • Team input from treating therapists and nurse case managers

Equipment Installation

From grab bars to complex lifts, we tackle projects of all sizes and apply the same careful attention to each installation.

  • Temporary, rental and long term
  • All lift types: vertical platform, stair, pool and ceiling track systems
  • Determine best lift system for functional and caregiving needs
  • Equally consider best functional design, budget and scope
  • Work exclusively with certified and licensed installers from manufacturers
  • Mindful of state-to-state variance in manufacturer’s licenses and certifications
  • Well-versed in quality product selection from reputable manufacturers with warranties

Full-Service Construction

With a dedicated construction services manager at the center of every home modification to organize, communicate, inspect and plan, the result is a better smoother finished product.

  • Work closely with licensed and insured contractors nationwide
  • Dedicated, experienced project manager leads project from estimate to completion
  • Building, engineering and project management experience
  • Constant observance of project scope, schedule and budget
  • Project manager makes on-site visits when warranted
  • Regular detailed reports to the carrier throughout process
  • Communicate carrier’s terms and contractor’s abilities to injured worker
  • Increase accountability with the contractor, minimize overselling or deviating from scope
